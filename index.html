Coming Soon

So I decided to make an intentionally "ugly" looking blog in order to have some sort of creative outlet, because I really like writing and I don't have many excuses to do it.

What may or may not end up here:

  • Peeks into the mind of Jacob
  • Short Stories
  • Poems
  • Book Review like things
  • Essays on interesting but also dumb things
  • Other random stuff
  • And whatever else blogs are "supposed" to have

Really I don't know. This site is to be a call-back to the old days of the internet before it was dominated by multi billion dollar corporations. The world wide web was once more like the wild west.

I think this might be fun, but I first have to perfect my brand of "ugly". If everyone else stopped making websites using just HTML and CSS, I don't have to jump off that cliff with them. I don't care for "modern" web design, I care for fun things.

But a cliff I should jump off is some terms of service. While I craft some sort of official looking parody of a terms of service page I must say that by using this, wait, I can make this look more official . . .
